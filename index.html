🗺️ Krakow Family Itinerary

Aug 3–5, 2026  |  Toddler-friendly  |  Back by 19:30 daily

📅 Day 1 – Monday, Aug 3

10:30 – Arrive & Lunch  Przystanek Pierogarnia (Rynek)
Fruit pierogi (sweet with strawberries or blueberries) – toddler favorite. Quick, cheap, open 24h.
📍 Open in Google Maps
12:00 – Explore Rynek Główny  Main Market Square
Watch pigeons, hear the hourly bugle call (hejnał) from St. Mary's Basilica, peek at the Cloth Hall (Sukiennice) for wooden toys, optional climb of Town Hall Tower (free Mondays 11–15:00).
📍 Open in Google Maps
13:30 – Wawel Hill & Dragon's Den  Wawel Castle
Free to wander the courtyards and gardens. Visit the Dragon's Den (Smocza Jama) – a limestone cave with a fire-breathing statue. Great for kids' imagination.
📍 Open in Google Maps
15:00 – Planty Park / Vistula Embankment  Planty Park
Stroll or let the toddler nap in the green ring around Old Town. Wide, flat paths and views of Wawel from the Vistula riverbank.
📍 Open in Google Maps
16:30 – Early Dinner  Hevre Café (Kazimierz)
Set inside the old synagogue, varied menu with kid-friendly options (pasta, pancakes). Quiet atmosphere.
📍 Open in Google Maps

📅 Day 2 – Tuesday, Aug 4

10:00 – Kazimierz Stroll  Plac Nowy
Heart of the Jewish Quarter. Plac Nowy hosts a flea market (stalls even on weekdays). Grab a zapiekanka from the okrągłak hall.
📍 Open in Google Maps
11:30 – Lunch – Zapiekanka  Okrąglak Hall
Classic open-faced baguette with mushrooms and cheese, or plain for the toddler. Cheap, filling, and beloved by kids.
📍 Open in Google Maps
13:00 – Playground / Vistula Embankment  Vistula Boulevard
Multiple free, safe playgrounds near cafes. Flat promenade along the river is perfect for a stroller walk.
📍 Open in Google Maps
14:30 – Manggha Museum  Manggha Museum (Japanese Art)
Interactive exhibits, origami workshops, colorful displays. Free on Tuesdays (10:00–18:00) for the main gallery.
📍 Open in Google Maps
16:30 – Dinner in Kazimierz  Przystanek Pierogarnia (Kazimierz)
Return to the pierogi standby; kids love the sweet fruit varieties. Casual, quick service.
📍 Open in Google Maps

📅 Day 3 – Wednesday, Aug 5 (Departure)

10:00 – St. Francis Basilica  Bazylika św. Franciszka
Stunning Art Nouveau stained glass and peaceful interior. Quick and stroller-friendly.
📍 Open in Google Maps
11:30 – Lunch – Pierogi & Kotlet Schabowy  Przypiecek
24/7 pierogi bar; sweet fruit pierogi for the toddler, traditional pork schnitzel for adults.
📍 Open in Google Maps
12:30 – Playground – Skałki Twardowskiego  Park Linowy
Outdoor ropes course with low-level sections for young kids (supervised). Lots of wooded space to explore.
📍 Open in Google Maps
15:30 – Final Snack – Good Lood Ice Cream  Good Lood
Award-winning artisan ice cream; try fruit sorbets (dairy-free) or classic vanilla. A happy treat before departure.
📍 Open in Google Maps
17:30 – 🚗 Car Pickup
Meet your driver at the accommodation (or agreed point) and head to the airport or next destination.

🧭 Tips & Tricks

  • 🚆 Train from airport (PLN 17) – avoid taxis
  • 📱 Use Bolt app – skip rank taxis
  • 👶 Lightweight stroller recommended (cobblestones in places)
  • 🎫 Validate tram tickets if you ride
  • ⚠️ Avoid strip-club promoters near Rynek (scams)
  • 📅 Book Auschwitz/Wieliczka weeks ahead if you plan later
  • 👨‍👩‍👧 Dining is very family-friendly; staff welcoming to kids

🏠 Stay suggestion: Book an apartment in Old Town or Kazimierz (walkable, kitchen, ~$120–180/night).
